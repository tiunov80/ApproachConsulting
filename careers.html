Tampa-based Advisory, Business Optimization & Change Integration Consulting Firm seeks candidates for its Digital Media Internship program

Overview

Firm Profile: Boutique national consulting organization providing niche Advisory, Optimization & Change Integration services to clients from Private to Fortune 50.

Client Sectors: Financial Services, Healthcare, Staffing, and Technology

Challenge: Accelerate, Execute and Enhance Firm’s Digital Media Program, to ensure continued explosive business growth trajectory.

Approach: Identify potential candidates who consistently demonstrate flair, creativity & thought engagement to achieve company objectives aligned to Digital Media growth.

Outcomes : Firm’s Digital Media strategic objectives achieved/exceeded, Internship candidate gains broad understanding of and exposure to Advisory & Consulting business

Optimal Candidate: College Senior in 4-year Bachelor/Master’s Business Program. Major/Minor in Digital Media, Social Media, or Marketing is preferred.

Challenge

A boutique national consulting firm providing niche Advisory, Optimization & Change Integration services to clients ranging from Private to Fortune 50, identified an opportunity to accelerate, execute and enhance Firm’s Digital Media Program, to ensure continued explosive business growth trajectory More specifically, the challenge requires the successful incumbent to develop, implement, and sustain a formal Digital Media framework, to ensure Firm’s Digital Media strategic objectives achieved/exceeded.

Significant challenges include:

  • “Do no harm” to in-flight transformation
  • Ensuring Digital Media outcomes are optimized to promote organic growth
  • Supporting a diverse workforce & client base, with varying degrees of Digital Media value understanding & adoption capacity
Approach

As the successful internship incumbent, collaborate with our organization & clients to:

  • Conduct discovery to fully understand company objectives and strategic goals
  • Understand advisory & consulting services’ value & marketplace positioning
  • Develop an aligned Digital Media framework & articulate scope, timeline, deliverables & value to garner trust & support
  • Partner with an tenured staff to ensure joint success for Program outcomes
  • Implement & sustain Digital Media Operations to support achievement of organizational goals
  • Develop & maintain effective staff, client, vendor & wider network relationships
  • Transition successfully implemented model to Firm ownership/staff/future Intern Program resources
Outcomes

Fully implemented Digital Media Operational model to help the candidate & organization:

  • Achieve & accelerate growth
  • Enhance & grow its relationships, client base, supporters, followers, advocates and partnerships

All opportunities will be based at our Headquarters in sunny Tampa, FL, with some travel to client facilities likely, as may be required.

Submit resumes to staffing@approach-consulting.com . We will contact all potential candidates who meet our criteria set.
